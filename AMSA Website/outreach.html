Community Outreach

Acholi Medical Students Association (AMSA) is committed to improving community health through outreach programs that focus on prevention, education, and early intervention.

Our Outreach Activities

  • Health education on hygiene, sanitation, and disease prevention
  • Maternal and child health awareness
  • HIV/AIDS and STI sensitization
  • Malaria prevention and control
  • Community medical camps and screening

Target Communities

AMSA conducts outreach activities in schools, villages, health centers, and internally displaced or underserved communities within the Acholi sub-region.

Impact

Through these programs, AMSA strengthens trust between medical students and the community while promoting sustainable health practices.

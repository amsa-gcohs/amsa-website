Acholi Cultural Heritage

Acholi Medical Students Association values culture as a pillar of health, identity, and unity. Through cultural engagement, AMSA promotes social cohesion and community wellbeing.

Bwola Dance

Bwola is a royal and communal dance performed during important ceremonies such as celebrations, peace gatherings, and leadership events. It symbolizes unity, respect, and collective strength among the Acholi people.

Ajere Dance

Ajere is a vibrant traditional dance performed mainly by young men and women. It represents beauty, love, creativity, discipline, and the cultural pride of the Acholi community.

Culture and Health

AMSA integrates cultural expression with health education to deliver messages on disease prevention, maternal health, and community wellbeing in a culturally acceptable and effective manner.

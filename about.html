About Acholi Medical Students Association

The Acholi Medical Students Association (AMSA) at Gulu College of Health Sciences is a student-led organization committed to promoting health, unity, and culture. Our activities range from community outreach programs and medical camps to cultural performances that celebrate Acholi heritage.

Objectives

  • Promote awareness of public health issues within the Acholi community.
  • Preserve and celebrate Acholi culture through traditional dances like Bwola and Ajere.
  • Foster unity among members, the community, and sister institutions.
  • Conduct community outreach and health education programs.

Leadership

Our leadership team is composed of dedicated students who strive to guide AMSA in achieving its objectives while nurturing professional and personal growth among members.

About Acholi Medical Students Association (AMSA)

Acholi Medical Students Association (AMSA) is a student-led organization at Gulu College of Health Sciences that brings together medical students of Acholi origin and friends of Acholi culture

AMSA aims to promote Quality health services, medical knowledge,unity and Community engagement while preserving and celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Acholi people

Our Motto

Health for All, Ribe Aye Teko

Our Objectives

  • To create awareness about public health issues within the Acholi community
  • To carryout community outreachs including schools, health centers and villages
  • To carryout environmental health canpaign in the community through mobilization and sensitization
  • To promote gender equality among all members in all aspects
  • To promote Acholi culture and tradition both inside and outside the institution
  • To strengthen unity among AMSA members and partner institutions

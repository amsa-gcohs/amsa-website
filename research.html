Research & Medical Camps

Acholi Medical Students Association (AMSA) promotes evidence-based practice and hands-on community service through research activities and medical camps.

Research Activities

AMSA supports student-led research projects that address priority health challenges in the Acholi sub-region, including infectious diseases, maternal and child health, and community health systems.

  • Clinical and community-based research
  • Data collection and analysis
  • Collaboration with health facilities and supervisors
  • Dissemination of research findings

Medical Camps

AMSA organizes medical camps to bring essential health services closer to underserved communities.

  • General health check-ups
  • Screening for common diseases
  • Health education and counseling
  • Referral to nearby health facilities

Partnerships

These activities are carried out in collaboration with Gulu College of Health Sciences, health facilities, local leaders, and partner organizations.
